Entities:


Organization (Portal Parent Company): It will manage all the working of the portal
HR Managers: Will update all the information about the job openings
Hiring Managers: Will decide the flow of hiring process
Candidates: Will apply for job vaccancies and submit their applications.


Business Operations:

Job Posting : HR managers will post the details of the jobs about the qulification criteria and all.
Application Submission: Candidates will search for the best jobs for them and will submit the application.
Screening: HR managers will shortlist the candidates.
Interview: Hiring managers will conduct interviews.
Feedback: Candidates will be asked to fill the feedback.
Result: Result will be shown to everyone on their personal profiles and if selected further proces will continue with the company.
